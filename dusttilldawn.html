Dusk Till Dawn by Sia ft Zayn

"Dusk Till Dawn" is a track by English singer-songwriter Zayn, featuring Australian artist Sia. Released on September 8, 2017, by RCA Records, the song is part of the Japanese edition of Zayn's second album, *Icarus Falls* (2018), with a radio edit included in the albumâ€™s 2020 reissue. Written by Zayn, Sia, Alex Oriet, David Phelan, and Greg Kurstin, and produced by Kurstin, the song's music video, featuring Zayn and actress Jemima Kirke, was also released on September 8, 2017. It was featured in the trailer for the film *The Mountain Between Us*. The single reached number five on the UK Singles Chart, topped the charts in seven countries, and was a top-ten hit in over twenty others, including Australia, Belgium, Canada, France, Germany, and Sweden.
