"Without Me" is a song by American artist Halsey, released on October 4, 2018. Halsey co-wrote the track with Brittany Amaradio, Amy Allen, and Louis Bell, with Bell and Dylan Bauld handling the production. The song incorporates elements from Justin Timberlake's "Cry Me a River," with Timberlake, Timbaland, and Scott Storch also credited as co-writers. Released by Capitol Records, it served as the lead single from Halsey's third studio album, *Manic*. The track hit number one on the Billboard Hot 100, marking Halsey's first number-one single as a lead artist and her second overall, following her feature on the Chainsmokers' "Closer" in 2016. "Without Me" topped the charts in Greece and was a top-three hit in Australia, Canada, Hungary, Malaysia, Portugal, Slovakia, Ireland, New Zealand, Singapore, and the UK. It also reached the top five in the Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Latvia, and Norway. The music video, directed by Colin Tilley, was released on October 29, 2018. The single achieved Platinum or higher status in eleven countries, including 8× Platinum in Australia, 9× Platinum in Canada, and Diamond certifications in Mexico and the US. It was the number-one pop radio song in the US for 2019, the tenth biggest song globally that year, and the twelfth biggest song of the 2010s in the US. Halsey performed "Without Me" on several prominent platforms, including The Voice, The Ellen DeGeneres Show, Saturday Night Live, the 2018 MTV Europe Music Awards, the 2018 Victoria's Secret Fashion Show, the 2019 iHeartRadio Music Awards, the 2019 MTV Millennial Awards, and the 2019 Billboard Music Awards.
