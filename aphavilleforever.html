Forever Young By Alphaville

Forever Young marks the debut studio album by the German synth-pop band Alphaville, first released on September 27, 1984, through Warner Music Group. The album was accompanied by four notable singles: "Big in Japan," "Sounds Like a Melody," "Forever Young,and "Jet Set." These singles played a significant role in the album's success, which saw it charting impressively across Europe, securing a place in the Top 20 in six different countries and reaching the number 1 spot in both Norway and Sweden. Following the success of "Forever Young," Alphaville went on to release their second album, "Afternoons in Utopia," in 1986. In a testament to its enduring appeal, the album was reissued in a special "super deluxe" edition in March 2019, offering fans a fresh look at this influential work.
